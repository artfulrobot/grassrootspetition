This forms part of petitions' URLs. For example if you had a campaign called Decolonising Tech and you gave it a slug of decolonising-tech, and someone created a petition called Ditch Master-Slave Terminology in Databases then their petition would be available at www.example.org/petitinos/decolonising-tech/ditch-master-slave-terminology-in-databases. It’s suggested that you use all lower case letters, hyphens to separate words, since this is easiest to read and type.
